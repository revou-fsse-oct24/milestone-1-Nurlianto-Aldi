The Ghibli Nook

The Ghibli Nook is a restaurant serving comfort food inspired by Studio Ghibli's animated films. From Ponyo's ramen to Princess Mononoke's okayu and Totoro's bento, you can savor it all here. Visit our hidden gems nook in the midle of the bustling city and enjoy your favorite Ghibli Character's meals.

Our Favorite Menu

Ponyo's Ramen

Dive into the ocean's depth with our Ponyo's Ramen. This magical bowl filled with a rich, flavorful broth that will transport you to the seaside town where Ponyo and Sosuke first met. Each slurp is like a wave crashing againts the shore, filled with the promise of adventure

Princess Okayu

Savor the simplicity and warmth of Princess Mononoke's Okayu. This comforting rice porridge is infused with the healing powers of nature, just like the forest spirit, San. Every spoonful is a reminder of the delicate balance between humans and natural world.

Howl's Breakfast

Savor a warm, home-cooked breakfast in the heart of Howl's Moving Castle. From fluffy scrambled eggs to golden-brown toast, each dish is prepared with love and magic.
